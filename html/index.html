Making White Rice:

Combine ${r} oz of rice with ${r*2} oz of water and ${r/10} Tbsp (${r/20} oz) olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cook for about 18 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • ${r} oz rice
  • ${r*2} oz water
  • ${r/10} Tbsp (${r/20} oz) olive oil

Making Sprouted California Rice:

For slightly al dente rice:

Combine ${r} oz of rice with ${r/5*8} oz of water or broth and ${r/10} Tbsp (${r/20} oz) olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice:

Increase liquid by ${r/5} oz and cook time by 5 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • ${r} oz rice
  • ${r/5*8} oz water (+${r/5} oz for softer rice)
  • ${r/10} Tbsp (${r/20} oz) olive oil
